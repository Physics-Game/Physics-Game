Welcome to The Wave Game!!!

How To Play:

The Goal: Put the wave into the goal

The Barriers: Reflector: yellow rectangle --> reflects the wave using angle of incidence = angle of reflection
	      Refractor (Shallow): light blue rectangle --> bends the wave away from the normal
	      Refractor (Deep): dark blue rectangle --> bends the wave towards the normal
	      TELEPORTER!! (in): red and green rectangle --> teleports to out portal
	      TELEPORTER!! (out): red, green and black rectangle --> the output
	      Goal: the green rectangle --> your objective
			  
Rules: Barriers can only be used once per level. If you miss the goal the level will reset.		  
			  
Summary: Its a fairly straightforward game that has 12 levels and is single player   Have Fun! :)
